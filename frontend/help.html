Help Center

Everything you need to know about using the EduLearn portal.

Getting Started

To begin your learning journey, simply create an account and browse our extensive course library. Once you find a course you like, click "Enroll" to get started.

Technical Support

If you're experiencing issues with video playback or database persistence, please ensure your browser is up to date. For persistent data issues on Render, please refer to our Database documentation.

Account Management

You can update your profile name and view your course grades in the "Profile Settings" menu by clicking your avatar in the top right corner.
