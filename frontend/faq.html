Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions.

Is EduLearn free to use?

Yes, EduLearn is a student project and all courses and materials are free for educational use.

How do I access my grades?

Once you complete a quiz, your grade is saved. You can view all your grades by clicking your profile avatar in the top right and selecting "Profile Settings".

Can I sell books in the bookstore?

Yes! Any registered student can list a book for sale in the Bookstore section.
